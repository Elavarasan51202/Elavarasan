Bise Bele Bhath

Ingredients:

    • 1 cup rice
      1/4 cup toor dal
      1/4 cup chana dal
      1/4 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
      1 small onion, chopped
      2-3 green chilies, slit
      1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
      1 teaspoon garam masala
      1 tablespoon oil
      Salt to taste
      Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

    • Instructions:

    • Wash and soak rice and dals together for 30 minutes.
      Heat oil in a pressure cooker and add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
      Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
      Add turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
      Drain the soaked rice and dals and add them to the cooker. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
      Add 4 cups of water and close the pressure cooker. Cook for 3-4 whistles until everything is well-cooked.
      Let the pressure release naturally. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
      Your Bise Bele Bhath is ready to serve. Enjoy!

    • Tacos

    Ingredients:

    • 1 lb ground beef
      1 packet taco seasoning
      8 small taco shells
      1 cup shredded lettuce
      1 cup diced tomatoes
      1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
      1/4 cup diced onions
      1/4 cup sour cream
      Salsa and guacamole for serving

    • Instructions:

    • In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
      Stir in taco seasoning and water (as per packet instructions). Simmer for a few minutes until thickened.
      Warm taco shells in the oven according to package instructions.
      Fill each taco shell with the seasoned beef.
      Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, onions, and sour cream.
      Serve with salsa and guacamole. Enjoy your Tacos!

    • Butter Chicken

    Ingredients:

    • 1 lb boneless chicken, cut into cubes
      1 cup plain yogurt
      1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
      1 teaspoon garam masala
      1 teaspoon chili powder
      1/4 cup butter
      1/4 cup tomato puree
      1/2 cup heavy cream
      Salt to taste
      Fresh cilantro for garnish

    • Instructions:

    • In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, chili powder, and salt.
      Add chicken cubes to the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
      In a skillet, melt butter and add marinated chicken. Cook until chicken is cooked through.
      Add tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes.
      Stir in heavy cream and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
      Garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve your Butter Chicken with rice or naan.

    • Sushi

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups sushi rice
      1/2 cup rice vinegar
      Nori (seaweed) sheets
      Sushi-grade fish (e.g., tuna, salmon)
      Cucumber, avocado, and other fillings
      Soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi for serving

    • Instructions:

    • Cook sushi rice and season it with rice vinegar. Let it cool.
      Place a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a clean surface. Lay a sheet of plastic wrap on top of the mat.
      Place a sheet of nori on the plastic wrap. Wet your hands and spread a thin layer of sushi rice on the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
      Add your choice of fillings (fish, cucumber, avocado, etc.) in the center.
      Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, applying gentle pressure.
      Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
      Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi. Enjoy your Sushi!

    • Creme Brulee

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup heavy cream
      4 large egg yolks
      1/4 cup granulated sugar
      1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      2 tablespoons brown sugar (for caramelizing)

    • Instructions:

    • Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C).
      In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it's warm but not boiling. Remove from heat.
      In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract.
      Slowly pour the warm cream into the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
      Strain the mixture to remove any lumps, then pour it into ramekins.
      Place the ramekins in a baking dish and add hot water to the dish to create a water bath.
      Bake for about 35-40 minutes until the custard is set but still slightly jiggly in the center.
      Chill the creme brulee in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
      Sprinkle brown sugar on top and caramelize it using a kitchen torch or broiler.
      Your Creme Brulee is ready to serve. Enjoy!
